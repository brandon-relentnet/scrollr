# Scrollr

Welcome to Scrollr - the ultimate Chrome extension that enhances your browsing experience by adding a live, moving ticker. This ticker isn't just any ticker; it's fully customizable, allowing you to stay updated with real-time information on sports, stocks, and cryptocurrencies. Scrollr is designed for users who want to stay on top of their game without missing a beat, whether they're monitoring fantasy sports scores, stock market movements, or the latest crypto trends.

## Features

- **Live Fantasy Sports Updates**: Connect your fantasy football league, set your matchup, and watch as Scrollr displays a personalized ticker with live, real-time scores and stats directly from your fantasy football league. Whether you're tracking your fantasy team's performance or keeping an eye on the competition, Scrollr ensures you're always in the know.

- **Multi-League Support**: Easily switch between multiple fantasy football leagues with just a few clicks. Scrollr's intuitive interface makes managing and monitoring different leagues a breeze.

- **Versatile Viewing Options**: With Scrollr, you can choose to display the ticker at the top or bottom of your screen, ensuring that you never miss any critical information or action. This flexibility allows you to customize your viewing experience to match your preferences.

- **Fullscreen and Casting Compatibility**: Scrollr works seamlessly while watching content in fullscreen mode or when casting to your TV. This feature ensures that you can enjoy your favorite shows, movies, or sports broadcasts without missing out on important updates.

- **Stocks and Crypto Tracking**: In addition to fantasy sports, Scrollr also supports real-time tracking of stocks and cryptocurrencies. Stay ahead of market trends and make informed decisions with live financial data at your fingertips.

## Getting Started

To start using Scrollr, follow these simple steps:

1. Visit [Scrollr's website](https://www.myscrollr.com/) and add the extension to your Chrome browser.
2. Connect your fantasy football league and set your matchup to begin receiving live updates.
3. Customize the ticker's position on your screen and what information you'd like to display.
4. Enjoy staying updated without ever having to leave your current tab!
