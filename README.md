# Scrollr 🚀

Welcome to Scrollr - the ultimate Chrome extension that enhances your browsing experience by adding a live, moving ticker. This ticker isn't just any ticker; it's fully customizable, allowing you to stay updated with real-time information on sports 🏈, stocks 💹, and cryptocurrencies 💰. Scrollr is designed for users who want to stay on top of their game without missing a beat, whether they're monitoring fantasy sports scores, stock market movements, or the latest crypto trends.

## Features 🌟

- **Live Fantasy Sports Updates** 🏈: Connect your fantasy football league, set your matchup, and watch as Scrollr displays a personalized ticker with live, real-time scores and stats directly from your fantasy football league.

- **Multi-League Support** 🔄: Easily switch between multiple fantasy football leagues with just a few clicks.

- **Versatile Viewing Options** 🖥️: Display the ticker at the top or bottom of your screen, ensuring that you never miss any critical information or action.

- **Fullscreen and Casting Compatibility** 📺: Works seamlessly while watching content in fullscreen mode or when casting to your TV.

- **Stocks and Crypto Tracking** 💹💰: Stay ahead of market trends with real-time tracking of stocks and cryptocurrencies.

## Getting Started 🚀

1. Visit [Scrollr's website](https://www.myscrollr.com/) and add the extension to your Chrome browser.
2. Connect your fantasy football league and set your matchup to begin receiving live updates.
3. Customize the ticker's position on your screen and what information you'd like to display.
4. Enjoy staying updated without ever having to leave your current tab!
